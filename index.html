Registration for the uiowa Brainhack is now open!

The 2018 uiowa Brainhack is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2018 that is organized by ... The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: May 2 - 6, 2018

Costs: N/A (probably free)

Location: Iowa City, IA.

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2018 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
